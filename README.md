# THE TRAIL

> Walk with someone.

A long-term feature film and life project.

## Core Philosophy

Every life leaves a trail.

The Trail is not about hiking.
It is about how people leave traces in each other's lives.

We do not chase the meaning of life.
We simply try to walk today's step well.

Don't chase the moment.
Be in the moment.

The audience should leave the film not wanting to hike,
but wanting to spend time with someone they love,
help a neighbour,
plant a tree,
or simply slow down.

## Creative Principles

- Truth before drama.
- Silence before explanation.
- Nature is a character.
- Presence is the story.
- Walk with someone.
