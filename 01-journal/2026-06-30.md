## Important Realizations

- The West Coast Trail should not be treated as a background.
- The trail itself shapes the story.
- Richard is not escaping life.
- The journey begins with a simple invitation and a simple answer: Yes.
- The film should not predict the future.
- The film should record the present.
- The most important moments are not necessarily the most dramatic.
- The film should preserve what stayed after returning home.
