# M-002 — Camper Morning Coffee

## Memory

Camper 早上起来，在礁石上冲了一杯热咖啡。

不是因为咖啡。

而是因为那一刻。

海浪。

晨光。

没有人催促。

不用赶路。

只是站在那里，喝着一杯热咖啡，看着海。

## Notes

This is remembered as a moment of presence rather than an event.

It should be filmed quietly, with minimal dialogue and no attempt to explain its meaning.
