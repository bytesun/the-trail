# D-015 — The Trail as a Living Series

## Discovery

The Trail should not be limited to a single film. It can become an evolving documentary series built from the authentic experiences of people who have completed the West Coast Trail.

## Core Concept

- One season per year (2026, 2027, 2028...)
- Each season documents that year's hikers.
- Episodes are structured around camps:
  - Gordon River
  - Thrasher
  - Camper
  - Walbran
  - Cribs
  - Tsusiat
  - Pachena

Each camp becomes a natural storytelling chapter where hikers from different backgrounds, directions and countries briefly meet before continuing their own journeys.

## Creative Principle

The platform does not recreate experiences with AI.

Instead, hikers contribute their own photos, videos, sounds and memories. AI assists with organization and editing, while the experiences remain authentic.

## Long-term Vision

Over many years, the project grows into a living archive of the West Coast Trail, preserving how different people experienced the same trail across different seasons.
